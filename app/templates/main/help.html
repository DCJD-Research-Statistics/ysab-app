Help & FAQ

How do I reset my password?
If you forgot your password, then email the DCJD Juvenile Research Team, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
Can I edit my submitted application?
Yes, you can edit your application. Go to the "Applications" page, find the application you want to edit, and click on the "Edit" button.
What should I do if I encounter an error while submitting my application?
If you encounter an error, first try refreshing the page and submitting again. If the problem persists, please contact our support team at daniel.pacheco@dallascounty.org with details about the error and a screenshot if possible.

Progress Reports

How do I access the progress report?
To access the progress report, go to the home page and in the YSAB Project Progress Report section, click on "Submit Report". Based on your selection for Reporting Interval (quarterly, bi-annually, annually), you will be prompted to select the appropriate progress report.

How do I view your progress report?
To view your progress report, go to the header bar and click on the "Applications" link to access your application table. Here you will find your submitted applications. Click on the green "Progress Report" button on the application you are interested in. You will then be taken to the "Your Progress Reports" page where all your reports are displayed.
