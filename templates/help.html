Help & FAQ

How do I reset my password?
If you forgot your password, then email the DCJD Juvenile Research Team, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
Can I edit my submitted application?
Yes, you can edit your application. Go to the "Applications" page, find the application you want to edit, and click on the "Edit" button.
What should I do if I encounter an error while submitting my application?
If you encounter an error, first try refreshing the page and submitting again. If the problem persists, please contact our support team at daniel.pacheco@dallascounty.org with details about the error and a screenshot if possible.
